# Business Problem

## Background

Large organizations receive thousands of job applications every month across multiple departments, locations, and hiring teams.

Recruitment leaders often struggle to identify hiring bottlenecks, recruiter performance, candidate drop-off points, sourcing effectiveness, and overall recruitment efficiency because data is scattered across multiple systems.

Without centralized analytics, decision-makers rely on manual reports that are time-consuming, inconsistent, and often outdated.

---

## Problem Statement

TechNova Solutions receives more than 120,000 job applications annually.

Despite investing heavily in recruitment, leadership lacks visibility into:

- Hiring funnel performance
- Recruiter productivity
- Candidate drop-off
- Hiring costs
- Offer acceptance trends
- Source effectiveness
- Skill demand
- Time-to-hire

As a result, hiring decisions are reactive rather than data-driven.

---

## Proposed Solution

TalentLens AI will provide an enterprise recruitment intelligence platform that combines SQL, Power BI, Python, Excel, and Artificial Intelligence to deliver actionable recruitment insights for HR leaders and executives.

The platform will automate reporting, monitor KPIs, identify recruitment bottlenecks, and generate AI-powered executive summaries to support faster and smarter hiring decisions.
