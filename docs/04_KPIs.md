# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

## Recruitment Efficiency

- Time to Hire
- Time to Fill
- Offer Acceptance Rate
- Hiring Velocity

---

## Recruitment Funnel

- Applications Received
- Screening Pass Rate
- Technical Interview Pass Rate
- HR Interview Pass Rate
- Offer Rate
- Joining Rate

---

## Recruiter Performance

- Hires per Recruiter
- Average Hiring Time
- Candidate Satisfaction Score
- Offer Conversion Rate

---

## Financial KPIs

- Cost per Hire
- Recruitment Spend
- Agency Cost
- Referral Cost

---

## Talent KPIs

- Source Effectiveness
- Department-wise Hiring
- Skill Demand
- Diversity Ratio
