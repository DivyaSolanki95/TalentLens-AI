# TalentLens AI

> AI-Powered Enterprise Recruitment Intelligence Platform

## Overview

TalentLens AI is an end-to-end recruitment analytics platform designed to help organizations make smarter hiring decisions using data analytics and artificial intelligence.

The platform enables HR leaders, recruiters, hiring managers, and executives to monitor recruitment performance, identify hiring bottlenecks, improve candidate experience, reduce hiring costs, and generate AI-powered business insights.

Unlike traditional dashboard projects, TalentLens AI is designed as an internal enterprise analytics product that simulates the work of a People Analytics team in a large organization.

---

## Business Problem

Large organizations receive thousands of job applications every month.

Recruitment teams often struggle to answer critical business questions such as:

- Why is hiring slowing down?
- Which recruiters perform best?
- Which interview stage has the highest candidate drop-off?
- Which sourcing channels produce the best hires?
- What is the average time-to-hire?
- What is the cost per hire?
- Which skills are most in demand?

TalentLens AI centralizes recruitment data and transforms it into actionable business intelligence.
